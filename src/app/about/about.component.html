About Online Skill Checker

The Online Skill Checker is a web application designed to facilitate the administration and delivery of online exams. It provides a convenient and efficient platform for both students and educators.

  • For Students:
    • Take online exams from anywhere with an internet connection.
    • Receive immediate results and feedback.
    • Track their progress and performance over time.
  • For Educators:
    • Create and manage online exams with various question types.
    • Track student progress and performance.
    • Analyze exam data to gain insights into learning outcomes.

The Online Skill Checker is built using modern web technologies and is committed to providing a user-friendly and reliable examination experience.
